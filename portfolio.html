SDfolio V1

    SDfolio v1 is my very first website, showcasing my journey and interests. It took me two weeks to complete this project, and I am thrilled with the results. The site is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, providing a solid foundation for my web development skills.

   The portfolio consists of four main pages:

  • Home: An introduction to who I am and what I do.
  • Highschool: Details about my high school achievements.
  • Hobbies: A glimpse into my personal interests and hobbies.
  • Contact: Information on how to get in touch with me.
   Creating SDfolio v1 has been an incredible learning experience, and I am proud to share it with the world.

FLANCO

    Flanco is my recreation of a market store, serving as my second web development project. It took two weeks to complete, and I utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring this site to life.

The website features six main pages:

  • Home: Welcoming visitors with an overview of the store.
  • Electronics: Showcasing a variety of electronic products.
  • Magazines: Displaying a selection of available magazines.
  • Careers: Information about job opportunities within the store.
  • About: Details about the store's history and mission.
  • Contact: Information on how to get in touch with the store.

    Developing Flanco has enhanced my skills and provided valuable experience in web development. I am excited to share this project and its functionalities.
